About Us

Building a website is an exercise of willpower. The bells and whistles of the design process are tempting to focus on, but compelling content is what makes a website work for your business.

There are few pieces of content on your website that are more compelling than your mission, vision, values, and team. And all of these elements are typically found on the About page of your website

Sports

High quality equipment for all sports is made available to students. Two full time coaches are on staff to provide guidance to students. All the sports facilities are utilized by students every day and also during competitions held for College functions viz., College Day etc. College Bus facility is available up to 6:30 PM on all working days to allow students to spend time playing sports.

Campus

The College has 70 spacious classrooms and 31 labs (all classrooms and labs are equipped with LCD projectors) Air-conditioned conference rooms An independent three storied Library building An independent four storied Student Centre (with canteen, gym, indoor sports, medical centre, placement cell) Auditorium (seating capacity: 1500, fully air-conditioned, equipped with WiFi and 4 high powered LCD projectors) Extensive outdoor sports facilities Hostel

Labs

The College has well-equipped labs (well beyond prescribed syllabus requirements in some cases). Please refer to individual department pages for more details.

Hostel

Our belief that a residential campus offers a superior environment for academics turned into a reality during 2009-10. Excellent and facilitative provision of Hostel accommodation is a major support service offered by CVR College of Engineering. Moving away from home to study is an exciting time in life.

Student Center

The Student Centre is an independent four story building which houses various facilities such as canteen, gym, indoor sports facilities, medical centre, and placement office.
